Oh... Jawaharlal
Non - aligned movement

The movement of non - aligned nations -a movement to protect the interests of developing countries and to safe guard them from being used as tools, -a movement ignored and neglected in the fifties but respected in the seventies.

When Pakistan applied for the membership in the Colombo conference of non - aligned nations you, the moving spirit of the movement were not physically present, but Oh Jawaharlal, your statesmanship was vindicated.
